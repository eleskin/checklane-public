# Roadmap

The goal of Checklane is to help small SaaS teams discover integration failures before their customers do.

## Planned

* Slack alerts
* Telegram alerts
* Better incident filtering
* Integration templates
* Response content validation

## Under Consideration

* Public status pages
* Webhook monitoring
* Self-hosted edition
* Team collaboration

## Not Planned Right Now

* Enterprise features
* On-premise deployments
* Complex observability tooling
